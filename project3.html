OnClimate - Student Education App

Role:

Designer (Team Project)

This was a team project for a class at UCSC, CMPM 131. We created an app design based off a prompt and spent a whole quarter making prototypes, iterating them and eventually coming up with a final design. The main point of the app is to give a better understanding of environmental issues to students and others who are interested. It includes social media aspects, a calendar feature, and a learn tab with articles and other media. The final result is a polished app with a modern style that allows users to seamlessly navigate the app and learn new things at the same time.
